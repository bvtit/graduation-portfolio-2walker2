Project Description

Study for, practice and obtain the COMPTIA A+ certification. This includes completing the Testout Labsim course and obtaining Testout's PC Pro certification

Tasks Completed

  • Complete Testout Labsim
  • Obtain the Testout PC Pro certification
  • Take and pass the COMPTIA A+ 220.901 certification test
  • Take and pass the COMPTIA A+ 220.902 certification test

To complete this certification, I first completed Testout's PC Pro Labsim course before taking and passing the Testout PC Pro certification test. I then reviewed and studied the material to prepare to take the COMPTIA A+ certification tests I then took and passed both the COMPTIA 220.901 and 220.902 exams to earn the COMPTIA A+ certification.
